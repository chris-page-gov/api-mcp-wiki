**Ambition:** the [Blueprint](../../uk-government/blueprint-modern-digital-government.md) (2025) — Digital Backbone, open API by default, "once only".

**Reality:** the [April 2025 discovery](../../uk-government/api-discovery-2025.md) found maturity *generally low*; the [API Hub alpha](../../uk-government/api-hub-alpha.md) showed demand for a catalogue, quality/security scores, role-based guidance, tooling and a signed-in experience.

**Key distinction:** *Open API = discoverable, documented, available under transparent conditions — not necessarily anonymous access or open data* (grounded in the [API Catalogue](../../uk-government/api-catalogue.md) caveat).

**Evaluation:** accurate and well-balanced; the policy-vs-maturity framing is exactly right.
