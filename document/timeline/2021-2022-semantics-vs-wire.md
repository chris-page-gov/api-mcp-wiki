[RFC 9110](../../standards/http-semantics.md) defines semantics; [HTTP/3](../../standards/http-3.md) maps them onto QUIC. **The enduring contract is the semantics; versions just carry it** — so *standardise durable semantics and outcomes, not infrastructure choices.*

**Evaluation:** accurate; one of the document's best conceptual points.
