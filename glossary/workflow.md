# Definition
A **workflow** is an ordered sequence of API calls, with their dependencies, that together achieve an outcome &#x2014; what [Arazzo](../standards/arazzo.md) describes. The document's example: &#x201C;tell government someone has died&#x201D; is a governed workflow, not one endpoint.
