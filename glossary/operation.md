# Definition
An **operation** is a specific action on a [channel](channel.md) &#x2014; sending (publish) or receiving (subscribe) a message. When AsyncAPI 3.0 &#x201C;**split operations out of channels**&#x201D;, it began describing operations *separately* from the channels they use, removing the old ambiguity about which side sends and which receives.
