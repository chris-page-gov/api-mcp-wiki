# Definition
An organisation that **develops, maintains and publishes technical standards**. The bodies behind the timeline include the [IETF](../organisations/ietf.md), [W3C](../organisations/w3c.md), the [OpenAPI Initiative](../organisations/openapi-initiative.md), [CNCF](../organisations/cncf.md) and the [Linux Foundation](../organisations/linux-foundation.md).
