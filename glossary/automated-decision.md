# Definition
A decision produced by **automated processing** rather than a person. Under the [Data (Use and Access) Act 2025](../uk-government/duaa-2025-adm.md), a **significant, solely-automated decision** (one with legal or similarly significant effect and no [meaningful human involvement](meaningful-human-involvement.md)) triggers safeguards: information, representations, human intervention and the right to contest.
