# What it is
The **OpenAPI Initiative** formed under the Linux Foundation (**Nov 2015**); Swagger 2.0 was donated; **OpenAPI 3.0 (2017)** followed. **OpenAPI 3.2** shipped **September 2025** (streaming descriptions, broader HTTP methods, hierarchical tags).

# In the document
The archetype of "prose documentation → executable contracts". UK guidance recommends specification-first development with OpenAPI.
