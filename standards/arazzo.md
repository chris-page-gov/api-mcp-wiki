# What it is
The **Arazzo Specification** describes multi-step workflows across OpenAPI- and AsyncAPI-described APIs, human- and machine-readable. **v1.1.0** adds **AsyncAPI support** and OpenAPI 3.2 alignment.

# In the document
Cited for "individual operations → whole outcomes" — e.g. "tell government someone has died" as a governed *sequence*.
