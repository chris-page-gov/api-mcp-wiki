# What it is
The **ATRS** is the UK standard for publishing how and why the public sector uses algorithmic tools. It is **mandatory across central government and ALBs** for tools that significantly influence decisions with a public effect or interact with the public, and was **added to the digital spend controls (February 2025)**.

# Why it matters here
Turns the document's "registry/inventory" idea into a legal-ish gate: agentic tools that make or shape public decisions need a published ATRS record before go-live. See [legal and accountability layer](../document/themes/legal-accountability-layer.md).
