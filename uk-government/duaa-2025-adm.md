# What it is
The **DUAA 2025** (Royal Assent **19 June 2025**) replaced UK GDPR Art 22 with **Arts 22A–22D**, in force **5 February 2026**. Art 22A defines a *significant decision* and *meaningful human involvement*; 22B restricts special-category data; **22C requires four safeguards** for a significant solely-automated decision — information, representations, human intervention/review, and the right to contest; 22D empowers the Secretary of State to set further safeguards.

# Why it matters here
This is the binding control behind the document's [agent-safety list](../document/themes/future-directions.md) — it determines when an agent may make a consequential decision and what the citizen is owed. See [The legal and accountability layer](../document/themes/legal-accountability-layer.md).
