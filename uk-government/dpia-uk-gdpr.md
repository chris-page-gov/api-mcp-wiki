# What it is
**UK GDPR Art 35** requires a **Data Protection Impact Assessment** before high-risk processing; **Art 25** requires data protection by design and by default. For agentic systems these are pre-production gates, not paperwork after launch.

# Evaluation
Stable law. Maps to the mandatory pre-production gate in the [legal layer](../document/themes/legal-accountability-layer.md). Relates to: [DUAA 2025](duaa-2025-adm.md), [PSED](equality-act-psed.md).
